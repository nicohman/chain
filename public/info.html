About Demenses
Demenses is a social network that focuses upon the content more than the users, thereby eliminating in large part the worst part of most social networks: people. Now, you can browse memes and news without anybody messaging, pestering, or friending you! It is designed to be a federated system with no single server, but that will be released when I am sure it will be secure. You can view the code at its github
